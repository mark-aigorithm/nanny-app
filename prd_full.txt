
NANNYMOM
Mobile Application
Product Requirements Document (PRD)



1. Executive Summary
NannyMom is a dual-sided mobile application designed to connect mothers with vetted childcare professionals (nannies) and with a peer community of nearby mothers. The platform combines on-demand nanny booking, real-time care monitoring, a social community feed, event planning, and a peer-to-peer marketplace — all within a single, cohesive mobile experience.
The application addresses three core user needs: (1) safe, convenient access to verified childcare professionals; (2) transparent, real-time visibility into child care activities while parents are away; and (3) a trusted social network of local mothers for advice, events, and peer-to-peer commerce.

10. Proposed New Features
10.1 AI-Powered Nanny Matching Engine
Instead of static distance + rating sort, implement an ML-based recommendation engine that scores nannies against a mother's booking history, stated preferences (age group of child, activities preferred), scheduling patterns, and language requirements. The engine learns from post-booking ratings to improve future matches.

10.2 Child Development Milestone Tracker
An integrated milestone tracker (powered by WHO/AAP developmental guidelines) that logs a child's developmental milestones and correlates them with care log activities entered by nannies. Parents receive personalized activity suggestions for upcoming milestones.

10.3 Multi-Camera Home Monitor with Zones
Extend the single-room live video monitor to support multiple camera feeds per home, organized into named zones (Living Room, Nursery, Garden). Parents switch between zones via a tab interface; motion alerts are zone-specific.

10.4 In-App Group Messaging for Events
When a mother RSVPs to a community event, automatically add her to a private group chat for that event. The group chat enables logistics coordination (directions, what to bring), survives beyond the event for follow-up, and can be converted into a standing playgroup chat.

10.5 Nanny Background Check Integration
Integrate with a third-party identity and background check provider (e.g., Checkr, Sterling) to automate the nanny verification workflow in-app. Nannies complete the check at onboarding; results are auto-applied to their profile badge. Annual re-checks are prompted automatically.

10.6 Recurring Booking & Nanny Subscription
Allow mothers to schedule recurring bookings (weekly, bi-weekly) with a preferred nanny at a negotiated subscription rate. The nanny commits to a recurring schedule; both parties receive automated reminders and the booking is auto-confirmed each cycle.

10.7 Marketplace In-App Payment & Escrow
In v1.0, marketplace transactions are peer-to-peer without platform payment processing. Proposed: add escrow-based in-app payment where the buyer pays via the app, funds are held in escrow, and released to the seller upon buyer confirmation of item receipt. Dispute resolution handled by support.
